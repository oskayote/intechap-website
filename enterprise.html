Delivery Options

We Come to You — or You Come Online

We offer multiple delivery formats to fit your organization's structure, geography, and schedule.

📍 On-Site at Your Facility

Dr. Rodriguez and our instructors travel directly to your organization. We set up in your facility, train in your environment, and work with your actual equipment where applicable.

Training delivered at your plant, office, or campus
Instruction uses your actual systems and equipment
Available throughout the United States
Multiple sessions or full week-long intensives
Best for hands-on, manufacturing, or lab environments

🌐 Live Online / Webinar

Our instructors deliver live training via webinar platforms. Your team joins from anywhere — a single room, multiple locations, or individual remote setups.

Flexible scheduling across time zones
Full live interaction and Q&A
Hands-on demos streamed in real-time
Ideal for geographically distributed teams
Recorded sessions available for replay

🖥️ Self-Paced eLearning (Group)

Deploy our eLearning courses to your entire team. Each member gets their own portal account and completes training on their individual schedule.

Individual accounts for each team member
Progress tracking and completion reporting
Scalable to any team size
No scheduling required — train anytime
Group pricing available

🔀 Blended / Hybrid

Combine eLearning for foundational theory with live sessions for applied practice. The most effective format for comprehensive, lasting skill development.

Structured eLearning pre-work before live sessions
Live coaching focuses on application and Q&A
Maximizes time efficiency for busy teams
Customizable ratio of online vs. live instruction
Recommended format for most organizations
