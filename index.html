Meet the Founder

OR

Oscar "Dr. O" Rodriguez

President & Founder · Academy Professor & STEM International Education Consultant

Dr. O's academic credentials span international teaching and consulting in engineering and technology. He holds undergraduate degrees in Engineering Technology (Automation, Robotics & Instrumentation), a Master of Science in Electronics & Computers, and a Specialist and Doctoral degree in Engineering Technology Education. He also holds professional certifications in electronics manufacturing, advanced manufacturing, fiber optics, renewable energy, robotics, and control systems cybersecurity.

Dr. Rodriguez has taught engineering and technology for nearly 15 years at corporate sites and college campuses, using online, distance, and computer-assisted instructional methods. He has developed and implemented numerous STEM projects across the U.S. — and loves sharing with lifelong learners around the world.
